    Grading Policy

    • Project: 45% of semester grade (15% per project). You have 72 slip hours, and after you have used up your extension budget, any assignment handed in late will be marked off 25% per day late, rounded up to the nearest integer number of days. No assignment will be accepted more than 4 days late.
    • Homework: 10% of semester grade.
    • Two Mid-terms: 20% of the semester grade total. 12% will come from your higher-scored exam, 8% from your lower-scored exam.
    • Final Exam: 25% of the semester grade.

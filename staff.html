    • Karthik Ramasamy
      OH: Wednesday, 5pm

      Karthik is the engineering manager and technical lead for Real Time Analytics at Twitter. He is the co-creator of Heron and has more than two decades of experience working in parallel databases, big data infrastructure and networking.
